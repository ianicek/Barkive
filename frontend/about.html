About Barkive

Barkive is your one-stop shop for managing all your dog's needs. From organizing veterinary records to accessing breed-specific care information and insurance planning. Barkive centralizes and simplifies pet care and helps you keep your furry friend happy and healthy.

Core Features

  • Dashboard for Pet Infromation
    • User prompt that provide information about next vet appointment, or next prescription refillment.
    • Provide a glance of user's pet information, including name, breed, gender, weight, medications, appointment, and veterinarian contact.
  • Medical Page for Records
    • Has all information about pet inluding name, breed, age, gender, weight, insurance used, vetenarian, medical conditions, and medications.
    • A containter that has insurance information such as provider, coverage, and website link.
    • Emergency contact information to 24hr vet which inlcudes the vets name, phone number, and address.
    • A container with veterinarian information such as name, contact info, and address.
  • Pet Breed and Insurance Research Page
    • Breed search funcationality that allows users to search by breed, personality, grooming needs, and access all realted meta data base.
    • In breed search provides recommendations for insurances and medications that would be beneficial for each breed.
    • Insurance search functaionality that allows user to search insurances by coverage areas and all related meta data base.
  • Profile Information
    • Display users infromation such as name, email, phone number, password, when account was created, and role (user or admin).
    • Be able to edit/updated users information.
    • A containter that all users pets and be able to add, remove, and edit pet information
